This low-maintenance sheet set has a great look and a fabulous feel.

 
  • quick-dry finish for less time in the dryer, lower energy consumption and faster linen changes
  • wrinkle-free cotton sateen for a freshly ironed look straight from the dryer
  • flexifit fitted sheet has just a hint of spandex for a smooth, snug fit; fits mattresses up to 15" deep
  • wide range of sophisticated colors to complement your bedding

Set includes:

  • fitted sheet
  • flat sheet
  • pillowcases

Twin set has 1 standard/queen pillowcase; full and queen sets have 2 standard/queen pillowcases. King and California king sets have 2 king pillowcases. Extra pillowcases are sold separately. Flat sheet and pillowcases are cotton; fitted sheet is cotton/spandex. Washable. Imported.
